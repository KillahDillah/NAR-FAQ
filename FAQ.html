Frequently Asked Questions

When will my story get published?
Contingent on quality and quantity of submissions, the average turnaround time is 6 weeks. As long as your story follows our guidelines, you should see it.

Why wasn't my story published?
If your story is questionable or against our guidelines, it will not be posted.

Why is my published story slightly different from what I submitted?
Sometimes slight edits are needed to increase readability. We want others to read your story so we reduce extraneous detail like brand names, 3rd person POV, offensive language, etc. while keeping the spirit of the story.

Is this site suitable for children?
We strive to censor profanity and other explicit content, but there are some situations not suitable for younger audiences.

Are your stories real?
Every selected story must undergo a key word search to ensure uniqueness. Past unpublished submissions have included quotes from TV shows, variations of YouTube clips, and amended tales from comedians. If you've read a published story that you've heard elsewhere, please contact us!

What is the meaning of life?
The answer to Life, the Universe, and Everything is 42.
