Who Are We?

Not Always Right is absurd & bizarre but true stories submitted by everyday people from around the world.

Originally launched in 2007, Not Always Right focused on illogical & ludicrous customer stories (“The customer is NOT always right”). However, Not Always Right has since expanded to include anecdotes from all spheres of life.

Not Always Right currently features stories about customers, bosses & coworkers, romance, family, school, friends & strangers, feel good, healthcare, and law.


Not Always Right is a small team of eight. Our staff includes five hard-working: editors, two tech wizards, and the OG founder. We are based in the US, Canada, and the UK.

Our Stories

Who writes these stories?

Who writes the titles of the stories?

The vast majority of the titles are cleverly conceived by our team of editors. Our own readers also welcome to include a suggested title when submitting a story.
