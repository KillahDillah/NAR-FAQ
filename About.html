About

What is this?

Not Always Right features absurd & bizarre but true stories submitted by readers from around the world. When we originally launched in 2007, the site focused on funny & stupid customer stories (“The customer is NOT always right”). However, Not Always Right has since expanded to included anecdotes from all spheres of life.

Not Always Right now features stories about customers, bosses & coworkers, romance, family, school, friends & strangers, feel good, healthcare, and law.


Who are we?

Not Always Right is run by a small team of seven gals & guys. Our staff includes five hard-working editors, one tech wizard, and the original founder. We are based in the US, Canada, and the UK.

Our Stories

Who writes these stories?

All of our stories are submitted by readers just like you!


Who writes the titles of the stories?

The vast majority of the titles are cleverly conceived by our team of editors. Our own readers also welcome to include a suggested title when submitting a story.
