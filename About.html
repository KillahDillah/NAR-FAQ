Who Are We?

Not Always Right is a collection of absurd & bizarre but true stories submitted by people from around the world.

Originating in 2007, Not Always Right was founded after witnessing [insert story]. Clearly, the customer is NOT always right. With bewilderment and curiosity, Not Always Right was born which now features stories about customers, bosses & coworkers, romance, family, school, friends & strangers, feel good, healthcare, and law.


Not Always Right is a small team of hard-working editors, tech wizards, and the OG founder. We are based in the US, Canada, and the UK.

Our Stories

The stories at Not Always Right are submitted by our readers from across the globe. The vast majority of the titles are cleverly conceived by our editorial team but readers are welcome to include a suggested title when submitting a story.
