Guidelines

Story Content

Must be original!
If you weren't there to experience it, please don't submit it. Please no submissions of something you've seen on TV shows, watched in a movie, or heard from a comedian.

Nothing overly offensive or crude.
Some stories will offend (nature of the site), but we aim to keep it PG-13. Content including racial slurs, crude language, hateful comments, and anything demeaning will not be published.

Keep it weird.
We publish unique stories that leave us speechless, laughing, in a state of disbelief, or flat out puzzled.

Keep it simple.
Each story should only take a couple minutes to read. Provide enough content to get the point across without unecessary details.
